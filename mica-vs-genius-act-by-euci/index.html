MiCA vs GENIUS Act by the European Crypto Initiative (EUCI)

20 September 2025

I don't normally spend my time reading up on foreign regulatory developments, but analogous to the proverb "When America sneezes, the rest of the world catches the cold", you really cannot ignore what is happening overseas.

European Crypto Initiative (EUCI) published a very succint and clear comparison between the two regulations: MiCA vs. GENIUS Act (2025), covering the following aspects:

  • Legal & regulatory scope;
  • Stablecoin regulation;
  • Regulatory and industry impact;
  • Legislative and political process.

I came across the European Crypto Initiative through their excellent article series "Read legislation X with EUCI". They are currently my go to source for information on the latest developments wrt legislation of virtual assets. For more information see:

  • Read TFR with EUCI: A Practical Guide to the EU’s Travel Rule for Crypto
  • The Read MiCA with EUCI Booklet
