Ethereum Smart Contract Auditor's 2023 Rewind by Patrick Drotleff

27 January 2024

It is that time of the year again! @patrickd published his recap of last year's noteworthy security incidents from the Ethereum sphere, without all the CT drama:

https://ventral.digital/posts/2024/1/19/ethereum-smart-contract-auditors-2023-rewind/

He focuses on the technical details, in order for other researchers to learn about them and -especially- to make sure these vulnerabilites are spotted in future audits and reviews.

What a benevolent person, he probably is the living incarnation of the "sharing is caring" attitude in the Web3 community (plus, ask him a technical question, and you always get an elaborate, yet to the point, answer).

👉 Patrick's blog https://ventral.digital itself is a treasure trove filled with interesting content (incl. Secureum), make sure to check it out.
