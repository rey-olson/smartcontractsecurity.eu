Now

3 July 2023

This is a now page, and if you have your own site, you should make one, too.

Life

  • Summer is here!
  • My focus is on social interactions nowadays: meeting up with friends and spending more time outside.
  • I try to tone down my screen time, but it is not always easy (my smartphone is fucking poison, I swear).
  • I revisited Slovenia with some friends: four days in total of which two were driving (approx. 1200km each way). We climbed a mountain (nine hour walk, with 1800m elevation gain). It was pretty demanding, but an awesome experience.

Family

  • Family life -by far- asks the most of my attention and that is totally okay.
  • Spending time with my kids (and wife of course) is great, but I cannot overstate it: raising children is quite the challenge.
  • Another school year has ended, all three did well.
  • We will spend our summer holidays in the mountains in Austria (with stop-overs in Legoland Germany & Technik Museum Speyer). My offspring is finally capable of longer hikes and climbs, so that is a welcome change.

Reading

  • I almost finished Cixin Liu's trilogy: The Three-Body Problem, The Dark Forest and Death's End. Still two-hundred pages to go, but I need a couple of hours of quality time to properly finish the series.¬†
  • Started in Recapture the Rapture by Jamie Wheal. Heard an episode about the book on Your Undivided Attention (podcast series).
  • I definitely need to stop reading books in parallel, it is a bad habit. On the other hand: FOMO is real, it is hard to commit to only one book at a time.

Work

  • I try to commute by bicycle as much as I can (approx. one hour one way), it is¬† probably a significant contributor to my well-being (and sanity).
  • Every time I think I truly understand something information security related, something else pops up and changes my view. The more I learn, the more I realize how much I don't know.

Web3

  • I am still struggling to spend time on writing and/or posting articles, but I am mostly at ease with how it currently goes (it ebbs and flows).
  • I have the opportunity to give a short talk on blockchain & smart contract security at a brand new security conference: Annacon. Contemplating about what topic(s) to present, and how to keep it entertaining.

Thank you Candost Dagdeviren, for the inspiration.
