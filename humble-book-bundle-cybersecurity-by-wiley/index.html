Humble Book Bundle: Cybersecurity by Wiley

2 February 2022

I try not to stray too much from the main subject of this blog, but then again blockchain security ís a broad field. It is always valuable to expand your horizon: cryptography, threat modeling, offensive security, ... You should try to at least grasp the basics. I leave it up to you to decide how deep you want to dive 🐋 😉.

Another year, another Humble Book Bundle about cyber security! This year's edition is offered by Wiley.

I'm too lazy to describe it any better than they do on their website, so here it goes:

"Master the art of digital defense
Learn to safeguard your systems and protect against online threats with our latest bundle from Wiley! Within the digital pages of these books, you’ll discover how to think the way hackers do and find tried-and-true techniques to put your cybersecurity measures to the test. Plus, your purchase helps support Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF)!"

At the time of writing the bundle already raised € 46,248.57 for charity!
